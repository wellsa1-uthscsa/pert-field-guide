# PERT Field Guide

Welcome to the Psychiatry Epidemiology Research Training Program Field Guide.

This guide supports students, residents, and early investigators learning how to ask rigorous clinical research questions, design EHR-based studies, interpret results responsibly, and communicate findings clearly.
