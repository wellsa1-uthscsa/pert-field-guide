# PERT Field Guide

*A Practical Introduction to Psychiatric Epidemiology and Population Health Research*

Welcome to the Psychiatry Epidemiology Research Training Program Field Guide.

This guide supports students, residents, graduate students, fellows, and early-career investigators learning how to ask meaningful research questions, define concepts carefully, interpret evidence responsibly, think across populations, and communicate scientific findings clearly.

## Suggested Reading Path

If you are new to research, begin with the Introduction and proceed through the chapters in order. Each chapter builds on concepts introduced earlier in the handbook and includes a reading assignment designed to help you apply the ideas in practice.

The appendices are intended as quick-reference resources and can be consulted as needed throughout the program.
